# Privacy Policy for Aptip

**Last updated: April 21, 2026**

Aptip ("the app") is developed and maintained by Andreas Berg ("we", "us", "our"). This privacy policy explains how the app handles information when you use it.

---

## Summary

**Aptip does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.** All data stays on your device.

---

## Information the app accesses

### Camera and Photos
When you use the receipt scanner feature, the app accesses your camera or photo library to capture an image of your receipt. This image is processed **entirely on your device** using Google ML Kit Text Recognition (an on-device machine learning library). No photo or image data is ever sent to any server or third party.

### Location
The app can optionally access your device's location to suggest the country you are currently in, so it can show you relevant tipping guidelines. Your location is used only in the moment of the request and is **never stored, logged, or transmitted** anywhere.

### Internet (Exchange Rates)
The app fetches current currency exchange rates from a public, anonymised API (`cdn.jsdelivr.net`). This request contains no personal information — it is equivalent to visiting a public webpage.

---

## Data stored on your device

The app stores the following data **locally on your device only**, using your device's built-in storage (AsyncStorage). This data is never uploaded:

- Your settings and preferences (home currency, language, defaults)
- Your calculation history
- Trip and bill records you create in the Split & Trips feature
- Participant names you have previously entered (for autocomplete suggestions)

You can delete all stored data at any time via Settings → Clear archive.

---

## Feedback

If you choose to tap "Send feedback", the app opens your device's email client with a pre-filled recipient address (`andreas.berg@gmail.com`). The app itself does not transmit anything — you are in full control of whether to send the email and what it contains.

---

## Advertising

The app displays a self-promotional banner linking to an in-app donation screen. There are **no third-party advertising SDKs**, no tracking pixels, no cookies, and no behavioural profiling of any kind.

Making a donation removes the banner. No payment data is processed by the app — any payment flow uses the platform's native payment infrastructure (Google Play Billing).

---

## Children's Privacy

Aptip does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13. The app is intended for use by adults.
